Community Day

Discover the exhilarating highlights of the 2024 AWS Student Community Day! Across February 15 and 16, at St. Joseph's Group of Institutions, we unleashed a powerhouse event, driven by AWS Cloud Placement Empowerment Program and AWS Cloud Club St. Joseph's Group of Institutions Drawing over 2000 enthusiasts, including renowned luminaries from the advocacy team like Jen Looper and Aditi Sawhney, alongside a diverse mix of 500 dynamic students and 300 industry trailblazers, the atmosphere crackled with energy and innovation. Featuring three electrifying tracks—security & career guidance, serverless & DataOps, and Gen-AI and machine learning , the stage was set with over 40 captivating presenters. From eye-opening sessions to priceless networking, the 2024 AWS Student Community Day was a beacon of inspiration and growth. Don't miss out on reliving the excitement!
